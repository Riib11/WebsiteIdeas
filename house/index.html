Humanity House

Turning Dreams into Reality

Overview

Humanity House was conceived as a way to improve the future of humanity and to assist students interested in the future of humanity. Starting in the summer of 2017, Humanity House will offer the following opportunities for Reed students:

  • Academic Resources - Humanity House associates will have access to our resources for research on the future of humanity, including an online reference collection, The Imps, and traditional book collections in Portland and Palo Alto.
  • Entrepreneurship Experience - Humanity House associates can develop entrepreneurial skills by assuming responsible roles in projects sponsored by our parent organization, The YouSA,.
  • Housing - Humanity House looks forward to providing accommodation for up to four Reed students a short walk from the campus.
